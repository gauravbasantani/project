Take your first steps

In order to guarantee a smooth journey to the U.S., we suggest that you begin your pre-arrival tasks as soon as possible. You must complete the following pre-arrival steps before you can register for your Global Launch classes:

1. Apply for your student Visa

You should now start preparing for the visa process, including applying for the visa and scheduling a visa interview. Visit our “SEVIS and Student Visa” page for detailed visa application steps.

2. Activate Your MyASU Account and ASU Email

By now, you should have received an email with your ASU student ID number, ASURITE ID, activation code and instructions. Please follow the steps in the email to activate your ASU email account, or visit our ASU email activation page.

3. Pay your enrollment deposit

Your enrollment deposit is used to secure your place in our program and must be submitted before you can register for classes. For more details and steps on how to pay, visit our enrollment deposit page.

if you are a sponsored student, you do not need to pay the enrollment deposit.

What's next?

You will continue to receive important emails in the coming weeks. These emails will include other important steps you must take before you arrive in the U.S., including securing your housing, registering for classes and more.

In the meantime you can also find more detailed pre-arrival information on My GL. Just log in and click “Pre-Arrival information” at the top of the page.

If you have any questions, please email us at globalaunch@asu.edu or visit our Virtual Welcome Desk Monday-Friday from 8-5 p.m. Arizona time (UTC -7). We have many representatives who speak various languages.
